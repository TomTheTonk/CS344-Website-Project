Who We Are

Here at Advanced Manufacturing we pride ourselves in creating an environment combining effiency while not forgetting about our employees We are headquartered in Isle Royale National Park, Michigan with roots around the United States and the globe. We start as a small company out of an old warehouse with only five employees, a dream and a plan to get there.

Our Mission

We strive to help create effective solutions for our partners and clients while not forgetting our values in fairly rewarding employees for their contribution to our company. We offer a 401k, amazing health insurance, and three weeks paid vacation for all employees regardless of employement level from janitors to the CEO. We give back to our communities through our local give back programs offering child care services for employees and relatives.
