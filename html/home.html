Who We Are

Here at Advanced Manufacturing we pride ourselves in creating an environment combining efficiency while not forgetting about our employees. We are headquartered in Isle Royale National Park, Michigan, with roots around the United States and the globe. We started as a small company out of an old warehouse with only five employees, a dream, and a plan to get there.

Everyone is welcome to come visit our facilities for tours and information on job openings at An Address St., 50510, Isle Royale National Park, MI. You can also view our openings here.

Our Mission

We strive to help create effective solutions for our partners and clients while not forgetting our values in fairly rewarding employees for their contribution to our company. We offer a 401k, amazing health insurance, and three weeks paid vacation for all employees regardless of employment level from janitors to the CEO. We give back to our communities through our local give-back programs offering child care services for employees and relatives.
